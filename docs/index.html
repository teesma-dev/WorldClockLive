Features

🌆

Add up to 4 cities

Monitor up to four time zones at once in a clean, focused layout — no clutter.

🕐

Menu bar clock

A live clock lives right in your macOS menu bar — always visible, zero distraction.

↕️

Drag & drop cities

Drag a city from the city list and drop it into your clock list or menu bar — and drag it back just as easily.

🏷️

Custom label

Set a personal label for each city in both the clock list and the menu bar — so you always know exactly which time you're looking at.

🖥️

Display tab

A dedicated Display tab to fine-tune how your clock looks and behaves across the app and menu bar.

✨

Clean & minimal UI

Designed to stay out of your way — refined, distraction-free, and fast.
