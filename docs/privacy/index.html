Privacy Policy

Your data, not ours

Effective date: 5 May 2026

World Clock Live does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app runs entirely on your Mac — no analytics, no tracking, no third-party services, no network calls.

What we collect

Nothing.

World Clock Live operates as a self-contained macOS menu bar app. Your selected cities, custom labels, and display preferences are stored locally on your device using standard Apple frameworks (UserDefaults) and are never read, copied, or transmitted by us.

Third-party services

None.

The app contains no analytics SDKs, advertising libraries, crash reporters, or any other third-party tracking tools.

Network access

The app does not use the network.

All time zone calculations are performed locally using the IANA time zone database that ships with macOS. No connection to any server is initiated by World Clock Live.

Cookies and tracking

World Clock Live is a native macOS app and does not use cookies, web beacons, fingerprinting, or any cross-app tracking technologies. The "Required Reason API" categories declared in the app's privacy manifest are limited to UserDefaults for storing your preferences locally.

Children's privacy

World Clock Live is suitable for users of all ages. Because the app collects no personal data whatsoever, it complies with the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the equivalent provisions of the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR-K) by design.

Your rights

Privacy regulations such as the GDPR and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) grant you rights to access, correct, delete, or export personal data held about you. Because we do not hold any data about you, there is nothing to access, correct, delete, or export. Uninstalling the app removes any locally stored preferences from your device.

Changes to this policy

If this policy is ever updated, the revised version will be posted at this same URL with a new effective date. Material changes will also be noted in the app's release notes.
